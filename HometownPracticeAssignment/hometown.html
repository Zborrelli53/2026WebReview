About Canton

Canton is a small rural town in upstate New York in St. Lawrence County. There are two colleges within the town. St. Lawrence University (slu) and SUNY Canton.

Canton is relitivley close to the Canadian Border. There are two towns that are about a 30 minute drive from Canton where Canadian borders exist. Those being Ogdensburg and Massena, NY.
